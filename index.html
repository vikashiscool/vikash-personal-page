a little about me

Hello.

I'm Vikash.

After spending ten years working intensively in healthcare research and program management, I decided to dive headfirst into the world of web development and design because I am excited by the idea of building new technologies that solve otherwise complex problems. As a graduate of the Web Development Immersive program and Developer-in-Residence at General Assembly, I have received the type of unparalleled coding instruction and mentorship that often differentiates mediocre web developers from great ones. I have worked on individual and group projects that have enabled me to apply and further develop my planning, wireframing, database development, and coding skills. Through these experiences, I have gained a strong understanding of the HTML/CSS, JavaScript, and Ruby programming languages; the Express, Rails, and AngularJS frameworks; PostgreSQL and MongoDB ORMs; and a wide range of web development technologies and methodologies that I am now excited to apply as a back-end or full-stack developer. I am also continuously teaching myself new web development frameworks, libraries, and tools that will allow me to create more engaging and interactive applications— a process that I plan to continue throughout my career.
